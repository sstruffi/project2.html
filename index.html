Where You At?
Where You Going?

This project is a visual exploration of me as a designer, focused on defining my personal taste, style, and design values. Through a series of four posters created using a mix of visual collage and generative AI, I explored my design philosophy, creative identity, and what I believe good design stands for. The work emphasizes bold visuals and storytelling, using GenAI as a creative tool rather than a shortcut. Each poster reflects a different part of my design thinking, what inspires me, how I approach creativity, and the principles that guide my work. Together, the series functions as a visual self-portrait, capturing where I am now as a designer and where I want my work to go.
